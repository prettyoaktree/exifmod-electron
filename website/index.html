EXIFmod is a desktop app for batch-editing photo EXIF metadata using a preset catalog — camera, lens, film stock, author, and more. Optional Lightroom Classic integration and AI assistance for descriptions and keywords.

Download EXIFmod
HOW IT WORKS

From folder to finished metadata in seconds.

EXIFmod is built around a simple, repeatable workflow.

1

Open a folder

Point EXIFmod at any folder of images. It reads existing EXIF values immediately. You can also open a folder directly from Lightroom Classic.

2

Select files & apply presets

Pick images, then choose your camera, lens, film stock, and author presets from your catalog.

3

Add descriptions & keywords

Write them manually, or let a local AI model generate them from the image content. Requires Ollama. Review the product docs to learn more.

4

Preview & write

Preview every EXIF tag that will be written to your files, then confirm. Choose whether to create a backup of your original files.
